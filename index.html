Project Details.

Territorial Cup: Word Duel in the Desert is a game that pits ASU against UA in a battle for territory in Arizona, although this not the game of footballâ€¦it is the game of words! Each player signs up to play for their favorite school and then enters the game. Pick letters from ANYWHERE on the board to make a word, but be fast - you only have 60 seconds to find as many words as possible. Each letter is worth a point and there are bonuses for players who find more words within the time limit. Each board is randomized, so every game is unique! At the end of the time limit, your score is factored into your school's average. The school with the highest average score wins! The competition doesn't end there, though. Individual players with high scores are also recognized as champions for their school. This project was created in HTML5 and CSS3 using an MVC architecture.
